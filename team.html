Meet the Leaders Behind Dziko Lathu

Each member plays a critical role in driving our mission forward.

Joseph Peter Kamvabingu – Founder & Visionary Leader

Joseph Peter Kamvabingu is a visionary Malawian leader, youth entrepreneur, and founder of Dziko Lathu organization — a fast-rising grassroots organization committed to education, empowerment, and community development.

Born and raised in Malawi, Joseph Peter is deeply passionate about building a future where vulnerable families and underprivileged youth have real opportunities to grow, learn, and thrive.

Through Dziko Lathu organization, he has led multiple donation drives, supported school programs, mobilized volunteers, and inspired young people across regions to become change-makers in their own communities.

Joseph Peter Kamvabingu believes in practical leadership — one that listens, acts, and unites. His work reflects compassion, clarity, and an unshakable commitment to helping others rise.

He has empowered youth from urban townships to rural villages, proving that one young leader, driven by purpose, can spark national impact. Today, Joseph Peter Kamvabingu continues to lead with humility, courage, and an open heart for Malawi which is africa .

Faith Bwanali – Chief Operations Officer

Faith coordinates daily operations across all Dziko Lathu initiatives and ensures each program is executed with efficiency and care. Her leadership energizes the team and her passion keeps the mission grounded in service.

Emmanuel Kamvabingu – Finance Manager

Emmanuel is responsible for financial planning, reporting, and transparency within Dziko Lathu. He ensures accountability for every kwacha and supports sustainability through careful budgeting.

Gift Amosi – Community Outreach Coordinator

Gift builds strong relationships with communities, organizes local engagements, and represents Dziko Lathu in the field. He brings real stories, needs, and voices to the organization’s heart.

Blessings Peter Wyson – Marketing Strategist

Blessings drives awareness campaigns that tell the story of Dziko Lathu. Through content, posters, and community promotions, he ensures the message reaches donors, partners, and youth.

Kate M. Manda – Data Analyst

Kate tracks impact, analyzes program results, and offers insights to help Dziko Lathu grow wisely. Her data-driven mindset supports strategic decisions and long-term goals.

Timothy Chimbalanga – Social Media Specialist

Timothy crafts posts, graphics, and videos that engage Dziko Lathu’s online audience. He manages all social media platforms, giving the public a window into our everyday work.

Mbengo Gift Nthala – Youth Engagement Coordinator

Mbengo leads school-based programs and youth development efforts. His hands-on work keeps youth inspired, active, and connected to growth opportunities.

Tracy Chauya – Public Relations Officer

Tracy she is the Public Relations Officer of Dziko Lathu. She is passionate about community engagement, youth empowerment, and clear communication.
